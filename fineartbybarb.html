Fine Art by Barb

Role: Designer / Developer

Professional Work

Date Completed: June 2015

A teacher of mine introduced me to Barbara Chase who was in need of a new website. We talked back and forth to decide what it was that she needed and what her goals for the site were. She wanted something that showcased her work in a minimalist fashion. We decided we would make the work the focal point of the site. We worked together throughout the process to make sure she was getting just what she wanted.
