PNW Playing Cards

Role: Designer / Illustrator

Student / Personal Project

Date Completed: December 2014

In my final VCD class, we were prompted to design an entire deck of 52 playing cards. We were to come up with a new theme that didn't relate to any franchise. Living in the PNW I've always been amazed by our environment and all the wildlife that roams our woods. We also have a strong nack to love all things handmade. I decided to tackle a PNW themed deck of cards by illustrating the "royalty" of our environment.
