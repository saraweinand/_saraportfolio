Wedding Stationary

Role: Designer

Professional Work

Date Completed: March 2015

I was approached by a good friend of mine who was getting married soon. She asked me if I could create a unique set of invitations. She was having her wedding in the country, and she wanted her invitations to reflect the style of their wedding day. We decided on a unique handcrafted one of a kind feel for the set with a watercolor backgound and hand lettering.
