Meet the designer

Most importantly, you've seen what I can do, and if you've made it this far down my page, you're interested in knowing who I am. Let's hope im intersting enough for you to shoot me an email!

Hey there!

I'm Sara, a recent graudate from Eastern Washington University. I've completed 3 internships, tackled a few freelance projects, and spent every waking hour(I don't sleep much) immersing myself in design. Whether it's admiring the work of others, or working and reworking my own. I truely enjoy design and developing the web. The constant evolution of the internet keeps me excited. however, I also enjoy creating work that doesn't belong on the internet. I try to find inspiration everywhere in order to craft a unique and creative design. I believe when your are passionate about your work, you never work a day in your life. That's how i know i've chosen the right industry. When I find something I love, I jump in head first in order to soak up everything I can possible learn.
