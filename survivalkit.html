VCD Survival Kit

Role: Designer

Student Work

Date Completed: March 2015

As I was finding myself in a endless array of tight deadlines and lack of sleep, I thought to myself, I could really use a survival kit for designers right now. I decided to tackle the project and gear it towards new stuednt designers. The kit contains your essential tools for the field, a survival guide, caffine, and chill pills(gum). The survival guide is full of tips, tricks, and adivse for making your way though the VCD program.
